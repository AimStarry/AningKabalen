Who We Are

Built by Filipinos,
for Filipino Farmers

AningKabalen (meaning "fellowman" in Ilocano) was founded to give Filipino farmers a direct digital channel to their customers. We handle the platform, payment tracking, and buyer verification — farmers focus on growing great food.

Our reservation system lets buyers pre-book upcoming harvests, guaranteeing income for farmers before the crops even leave the field.
